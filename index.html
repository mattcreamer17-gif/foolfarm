The question worth thirty minutes is not whether BeFOOL works. It does. The question is what the shared-services layer underneath it looks like once portfolio company ten starts moving down the line while Xphere goes to enterprise GTM in the US.

Phase 01

The Eduba frame.

Most of what runs a venture studio is not AI. Sixty percent of the day-to-day is traditional software, database, and integration work. Thirty percent is rule-based logic: the gates between BeFOOL phases, the compliance checks a corporate partner wants to see, the KPIs that move a portfolio company from phase three to phase four. Ten percent is actual AI work, and that ten percent is where most studios waste budget because they put LLMs where Postgres would do the job faster and with better guarantees.

Our work is sorting which layer each piece belongs on, then making the ten percent actually ship.

60% Traditional software, database, integration
30% Rule-based logic, gates, KPIs
10% Actual AI work

The ten percent is where we focus. Sorting gets you there.
